Our Story

We built Macks because India's best snack — makhana — deserved a brand worthy of it.

Fox Harvest Foods was founded with a simple belief: that foxnuts — one of India's oldest superfoods — were being sold short. Loose pouches, generic flavours, no story. We changed that. Hand-roasted in small batches, packed in beautiful jars, Macks is what premium makhana looks like.

AS

Aaryan Singh

Co-Founder, Fox Harvest Foods

VG

Vedant Garg

Co-Founder, Fox Harvest Foods

Made in Darbhanga

Manufactured by The Healthy Crunch, Bihar — the heartland of India's finest foxnut crop. Sourced close to origin, roasted with care.

Small Batches Only

We don't mass-produce. Every batch is roasted to order, ensuring the crunch and flavour you get in jar #1 is identical to jar #1,000.

The Superfood India Forgot

Foxnuts have been part of Indian cuisine for centuries. High protein, low fat, naturally gluten-free. Macks just made them impossible to put down.

Building Toward Own Plant

We're scaling fast. A dedicated Fox Harvest Foods manufacturing plant is in the works — more control, better margins, same great product.
